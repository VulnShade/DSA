# Arrays

<h2 align='center'>Time Complexity </h2>

<div align='center'>

| Operation    | Time Complexity | Example (Python)           |
| ------------ | --------------- | -------------------------- |
|Lookup        | O(1)            | `array[1]`                 |
|Push          | O(1)            | `array.append('value')`    |
|Insert        | O(n)            | `array.insert(1, 'value')` |
|Delete        | O(n)            | `array.remove("value")`    |
</div>
