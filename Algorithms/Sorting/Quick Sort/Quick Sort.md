## Quick Sort
- O(n log n) Time,  O(n<sup>2</sup>) worst (if sorted)
- O(log n) space
- Picks a pivot
	- all less than pivot go to left
	- all greater to right
- list splits at pivot
	- new pivot selected
	- repeats

Best alorithm on average
Bad choice is there can be a bad pivot O(n<sup>2</sup>)
